Conference, Workshop & Orientation Design

Every event design begins with two questions: what is the theme, and who is the audience. In any academic gathering, the primary audience is graduate students and postdoctoral researchers. The programme is built around what they need to learn.

Each invited lecture is structured in two parts: the foundational paradigm of the topic first, then current advances. The default where speakers present group results to an audience without context is explicitly avoided.

  • Theme development and intellectual framing
  • Session and schedule design with learning outcomes
  • Resource person briefing documents
  • Conference website design and build
  • Programme booklet and abstract book
  • Orientation programme design for new students

Carbon Dioxide

View PhD Thesis ↗
2016 to 2024 · PhD Research

Activation of CO2 and Reduction to Value-Added Chemicals on Graphitic Carbon Nitride Surfaces

Led pioneering research on g-C3N4 materials for photocatalytic CO2 reduction and water splitting. Investigated 18 distinct g-C3N4 systems via one-pot, ionothermal, and supramolecular-assisted eutectic synthesis. Developed novel metrics including normalized surface concentration (NSC%) and light absorption efficiency per unit surface area (DP/SA). Introduced ML-assisted structure-property-performance correlation frameworks.

C3N4 Photocatalysis CO2 Reduction Water Splitting IIT Madras · CUSAT
Feb 2024 — Jun 2025 · Honorary Research Associate

CO2 to Sustainable Aviation Fuels: RWGS + Fischer–Tropsch Pathways

Developed comprehensive process concepts for CO2-to-SAF conversion via reverse water-gas shift and Fischer-Tropsch synthesis. Contributed to international research proposal consortia involving academic and industrial partners across Europe.

CO2-to-SAF RWGS Fischer-Tropsch International Consortia
Nov 2025 — Present · Independent Researcher

Industrial Decarbonization, Green Hydrogen & Net Zero Transitions

Interdisciplinary research in industrial decarbonisation, green hydrogen systems, and sustainable energy transitions. Exploring techno-economic pathways for Net Zero in hard-to-abate sectors. Developing AI-assisted educational frameworks for catalysis and energy systems learning.

Net Zero Green H2 Decarbonisation Bengaluru

Semiconductor Photocatalysis for Hazardous Dye Removal from Wastewater

Investigated solar-driven photocatalysis for industrial wastewater treatment. Studied advanced oxidation processes using semiconductor photocatalysts under natural sunlight. Explored TiO₂ and related systems for Rhodamine B degradation kinetics.

  1. Scope First
    Initial contact by email. A short exchange clarifies the project, content, timeline, and deliverables. No quote is issued before this.

    Pages, revision rounds, responsibilities, and exclusions confirmed in writing before work begins.
  3. Client Delivers Content
    All materials in organised, finalised form. Content collection work is billed additionally.
  4. Design & Deliver
    Work delivered to agreed scope. Agreed revision rounds included. Extra rounds billed at agreed rate.
